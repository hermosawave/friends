Empty Kyoto: First Day Pledges get us over 30% to the Goal

Hi everyone!

This was a crazy, great Kickstarter Launch Day, spreading the word to everyone; watching the pledges come in, both from good friends as well as people I don’t yet know. We got to over 30% of the goal. Yay!

To all of you, THANK YOU! We’re well on our way!

  • In response to a backer request, I added an add-on reward of a PDF copy of the book. If you’ve bought a printed book, you can now add a digital copy if you like.

  • I started the first baby steps towards the real book today:

Books are usually printed in 8 (or 16) page signatures. These are like 8 page brochures all bundled up together to make the book. In a 100 page book there would be 12 of these signatures (total 96 pages), plus the four pages of the cover.

One important note is that 4 & 5 of each signature are facing pages on the same sheet of paper, whereas the other facing pages are on different sheets of paper. Therefore pages 4 & 5 are the ideal location for a two page spread, a very wide photograph that takes over both pages. These would be the largest and most important photos in the book.

So one of my first goals is to find these twelve centerfolds. I’m hoping these photos will help create a structure for the book.

More to come!

Regards,

Daniel
