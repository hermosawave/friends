Empty Kyoto: We're at 40%!

But still a long way to go…

Thanks everyone for supporting this project! If you have friends that might be interested in supporting this book, please let them know about the Empty Kyoto project.

Today I want to share another photo of the Arashiyama bamboo grove. I find it intriguing how varied the grove looks during different times of the day and with different weather, especially considering it doesn’t really get direct sunlight.

This shot is from midday on June 2, 2020. I’ve never seen it so bright and sunny. Things were still really quiet then - this is before Japan’s domestic travel incentive started, “GoTo Travel”.

Enjoy!

Daniel
