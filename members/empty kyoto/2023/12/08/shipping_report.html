Empty Kyoto: Shipping Report

Hi folks:

After 10 days, we’ve shipped about 2/3 of the orders. Any reward of more than a single book has already been shipped.

Tracking the packages, I can see that the first, most time-sensitive ones have already been delivered. The other shipments have either arrived in the destination country, or are through customs and waiting for a flight out of Osaka’s Kansai airport. For the US, “destination country” often means Fairbanks, Alaska, so it will take a few more days to get to you.

If I have shipped your reward, you will have already received an email with a tracking link.

I have been prioritizing USA shipments up to now, the vast majority of rewards. Starting Monday, I will prioritize UK/EU shipments. Shipments within Japan will go last, as most will be delivered the day after shipment.

I have plenty of boxes, but one thing that has become problematic is obtaining enough intermediate envelopes to go into the boxes. I’m going to have to get more creative with the packaging!

Anyway, you’ll get your books soon! I hope you enjoy them. And don’t be shy sharing photos on social media, I love seeing them!

Have a great weekend!

Daniel
