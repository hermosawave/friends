Empty Kyoto: 3 Weeks In - and Over 80%

Thanks for backing my Empty Kyoto book on Kickstarter! Here’s the latest:

My project is over 80% towards the fundraising goal! The finish line is coming into view. Thank you.

I’ve been pushing the book into shape. I’ve got about 60 pages in usable condition so far. I’ve been trying to place the photos on the pages so there is some rhythm, a dynamic. I’ll need to go back and see if there is more to write. And edit the text, just like I’ve been editing the photos.

It seems like the book will be more than 100 pages. 104? 112? More? I don’t know yet. Anyway it has to be a number of pages divisible by 8, for the book making process. The cover is separate four pages.

So that’s where we’re at right now. I hope we can get over, or at least close to the fundraising goal in the next two weeks. It shouldn’t be that hard, yet to paraphrase the baseball guru Yogi Berra, “it ain’t over ‘till it’s over.”

Best,

Daniel
