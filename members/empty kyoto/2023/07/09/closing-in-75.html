Empty Kyoto: Closing in on 75%. The finish line is almost in sight!

Thanks for backing my Empty Kyoto book on Kickstarter! Here’s the latest:

Hey everyone, thanks so much for supporting the Empty Kyoto project, I’m grateful.

After a great weekend of supporters coming onboard, we are almost at 75% of the fundraising goal. Thank you, thank you, thank you!

I’ve been working on the book as well. I’ve been splashing pictures across the pages to fill them out and see if the vague idea in my head lays out on paper. I’m using a 3-column, 3-row, template for aligning each page. It’s like a tic-tac-toe board. A template like this makes it easy to line things up and space them out evenly. I used it on the sample booklet too.

I’m also liking the way photos spread across two pages of the book, like in this image above. I’m am concerned about potential photo misalignment, but printing companies do really, really well at keeping the pages lined up, so perhaps I shouldn’t be so concerned about it?

Anyway, things are moving right along. Thanks for your support.

Till next time, stay cool.

Daniel
