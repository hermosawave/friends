Sunday, July 23, 2023

Empty Kyoto: One Week to go...

The Kickstarter campaign is rolling to the end, next Sunday!

In the meantime, I’m working hard on getting the book itself into shape. I have about 90 pages laid out, as you can see in the printouts above. I’ve made many changes to the photos to be used, based on how they work together and the idea of some sort of a story.

I decided to lay out all the main photo pages first, with the idea that they would be inspiration for the introductory section. Now that they are mostly finished, I need to write that introduction; to explain why I was in Kyoto and what life was like during the pandemic.

Now I wish I had taken more photos documenting pandemic life, like the many closed shops with their signs that read,「臨時休業のお知らせ」”Notice of Temporary Closure.”

Anyway, that’s what I’m going to be working on this week.

More to come,

Daniel
